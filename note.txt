ARM have 2 interrupt source
    - Interrupt from GPU peripherals
    - Interrupt from local ARM control peripherals

3 types of interrupt
    - Interrupt from ARM specific peripherals
    - Interrupt from GPU peripherals
    - Special event interrupt

Specific interrupts
    - One timer
    - One mailbox
    - Two Doorbells
    - Two GPU halted interrupt
    - Two address/access error interrupt
